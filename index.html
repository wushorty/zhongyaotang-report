Week of Apr 6–12 hit ¥367,542, the highest weekly revenue. Peak day Apr 5 reached ¥78,113 (1,263 pcs), likely driven by promotions or holiday traffic.

◆ 中后期趋于平稳Mid-Late Stabilization

Apr 13–22 stabilized at ~¥68,000/day. Apr 18 saw 919 pcs, the highest in the latter half. Apr 22 closed at ¥48,313, healthy overall.

Distribution covers 9 cities across 5 regions. In-transit orders span Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, Chengdu, Hangzhou, Dongguan, Fuzhou, Wuhan, Shenyang.
